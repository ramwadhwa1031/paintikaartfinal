Paintika Art

Hello, My name is Vidushi and I am an experienced artist

I am an artist who focuses on documenting life using paint & my paintbrush. I believe it is so important for us to fill our homes with things that make us happy, which is why I started creating art and dressing my home with colours I love. Art can have such a tremendous effect on our emotional well-being, and I am really passionate about how art can uplift and inspire. I worked in an office for around two years, and I had all the tools to be creative. However, I was creating what the business wanted, instead of fulfilling my own artistic journey. So I made the scary decision of starting my own business as an artist. There has been moments of self-doubt, but the passion and determination I have to create overcomes that. It has been the best change I’ve ever made, and helps me feel fulfilled and happy every day. It is a huge honour to be able to create unique pieces of art for people to enjoy all across the world.
